About Cape Town

I am looking forward to my 3 month stay in South Africa. The country is still rebuilding after apartheid ended over 20 years ago. The legacy of Nelson Mandela is still etched in my brain, and I plan to explore when I get there. Facts about South Africa:

  • There are TWO Chinatown's in South Africa. One is "old", the other is "new".
  • A person of mixed descent is called "coloured".
  • Cape Town is the most popular tourist destination on the continent.
  • The African tribe Khoikhoi populated Cape Town before Portuguese settlers claimed it in 1652.
