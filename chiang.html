About Chang Mai

Chiang Mai is known for being the most beautiful city in Thailand. It is a tourist and expat hot spot. Many travel here and settle due to the low cost of living. Flip flops can be worn nearly all year as the temperture averages in the high 80's.

What my friends say I should do:

  • Take a short trip to Vietnam and Cambodia. I can't wait to see this!
  • Take a quick picture of the Grand Palace. (I don't like touristy areas!)
  • Take a cooking class at Asia Scenic, a Thai cooking school.
  • Ride an elephant. Anyone who visits Thailand rides an elephant at least once!
