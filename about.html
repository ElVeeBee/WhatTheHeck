Bri and Bo, known as "Givin Em The Blues", are the founders of Blood, Sweat, Tears & Ink. As community leaders in Berkley CA, they help to bring poetry, comedy, and music in a fun, comfortable, and inviting environment. Speak Easy, a weekly event, has always been something separate and apart from being 'just another open mic' and it's infectiously exciting to be a part of something so many people say feels like home. If you haven't had the chance to check out their events, you are missing out! See the Events page to find out more information on upcoming showcases.

Poets, Comedians, Singers, and Musicians are welcome to contact them to book a show and show the world what you're made of. >
