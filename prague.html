About Prague

Prague is the capital of the Czech Republic. It is the largest city in the country.My new home for the next 3 months is a municipal district called Prague 7. It is a quaint, family friendly place. There are coffee shops, restaurants and parks within walking distance. There is even an urban garden nearby! Here are a few facts about Prague:

  • Prague's twin city is Miami-Dade County, United States
  • It is divided into two sides by the Vitava river
  • The currency is the koruna (czk). 1 USD = 24 czk
  • The average high in Prague from July - September is about 70 degrees. The low is around 40.
